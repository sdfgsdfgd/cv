Design Spec v1 (for Pages or HTML later)

Goal: Elegant, professional, ATS-safe, with subtle ornaments and clear hierarchy. One-page layout.

Layout
- Page: A4, margins 14-16mm.
- Header: full width, name large, title beneath, contact line in small caps.
- Body: two-column layout (65% right for Experience, 35% left for Skills/Impact).
- Dividers: thin hairline rules between sections; a small diamond ornament centered on the top rule.

Typography
- Headings: Hoefler Text Semibold (or Cormorant Garamond if available), tracking +4 to +8.
- Body: Avenir Next Regular, 10.5-11pt.
- Accents: Avenir Next Demi Bold for section labels and dates.

Color
- Primary text: Charcoal #1F2328.
- Secondary text: Warm gray #6D6D6D.
- Accent: Deep teal #0E6F6B (use sparingly on section headers and ornaments).

Ornaments
- Top rule: thin line across page with a small centered diamond (filled teal) and short line breaks on either side.
- Section dividers: thin line + small dot ornament aligned to the left margin.
- Footer: minimal line with page number optional (if 2 pages later).

Spacing
- Tight vertical rhythm: 4pt between bullets, 8-10pt between roles.
- Keep bullets to 1-2 lines where possible.

Notes
- Avoid large background fills to keep ATS-friendly.
- Use subtle ornamentation only at margins or between sections.
